About the Game

Outer worlds is a kids game. Its meant to help kids learn maths, physics biology and chemistry in a fun and new way. This game features a variety of learning challenges as well as an intriguing story. We follow Ralph, a six year old with Autism Spectrum Disorder into his first year at school as he tries to incorporate new knowledge with the help of his imagination. In this demo Ralph is learning about math and logic looking out for opportunities to connect 4 discs.
